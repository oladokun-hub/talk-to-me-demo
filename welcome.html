John, Welcome to TalktoMee

What happens next?
  • You'll see a list of counsellors that can attend to you. Pick one of them
  • Book a session with whomever you pick
How do I talk with my therapist?

Communication with therapist is via WhatsApp. You can communicate with your therapist through WhatsApp chats, audio call, video call. You and your therapist will schedule comfortable periods to meet.

What if I don't like my therapist?

If you have issues with your therapist, or the therapist isn't being professional, just contact us on the contact us page and inform us of whatever the issue might be.

How much does it cost?

Talktomee is a free counselling website. We do not charge anyone. Notify us if your therapist or anyone is trying to charge you.
